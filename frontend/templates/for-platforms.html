Built for streaming platforms

Cymatic Seal is designed to be integrated into the creator pipelines of Spotify, YouTube Music, and SoundCloud so that artists can protect their work from AI training at the point of upload.

Why platforms should adopt Cymatic Seal

  • Protect creators — Give artists a one-click way to seal uploads so their music cannot be used to train voice clones or generative models.
  • Differentiation — Be the first major platform to offer built-in adversarial protection, strengthening creator trust.
  • Technical fit — REST API and batch processing slot into existing upload pipelines; certificates provide auditability.
  • Acquisition-ready — The technology is production-ready for licensing or acquisition to become your platform’s default creator shield.

Integration options

Cymatic Seal can be offered as a service to your creators in several ways:

  • API integration — Seal audio server-side during or after upload; return sealed file and certificate to the creator.
  • Optional toggle — “Protect this upload from AI training” in the upload flow; seal only when the creator opts in.
  • Verification portal — Host the open-source verification tool so fans and distributors can confirm a track is sealed.
